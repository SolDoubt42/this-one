Features

Hardware wallet integrations

Solcage securely stores your digital assets,
with direct syncing capabilities to the Phantom wallet for streamlined management and trading.

Performance-Driven unlock mechanism

+30% Portfolio Pnl Unlock
The cage unlocks when your total portfolio achieves at least a 30% profit-and-loss (PnL) gain for the day.
Trading consistency unlock
Alternatively, it unlocks when your trading win rate exceeds 60% across a minimum of 5 trades in a day.

Sync with Phantom Wallet

Real time syncing with your phantom wallet
ensures accurate tracking of your portfolio's performance and trading results.
